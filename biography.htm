Biography


Fong Ming Lun, Alan (方銘麟) obtained his Phd (2015) in De Montfort University, Leicester, UK, Master’s degree (2004), and Bachelor’s degree (1996) in The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. He devoted himself to research & education work integrated with over fifteen practical experiences in the building services consultancy and contracting industry. He is an Instructor in City University of Hong Kong now of the Division of Building Science & Technology under the College of Science and Technology since August 2007.
