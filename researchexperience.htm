Research Experience

Mr Fong started research work related to thermal comfort survey and field measurement work related to energy saving aspects since 2009 while receiving Divisional Research Grant (DRG) and engaged in research degree course in De Montfort University, Leicester, UK since 2010. He is a supporting member of the BEETRU (Building Energy and Environmental Technology Research Unit) of City University of Hong Kong and involved in different consultancy work to resolve various building services engineering problems.
